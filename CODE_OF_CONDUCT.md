# Code Of Conduct

GitWhisper aims to be a welcoming project for contributors at different experience levels.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and patient.
- Assume good intent while still being clear about problems.
- Keep feedback focused on code, docs, design, and user impact.
- Help newcomers understand the project without gatekeeping.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Sharing private information without consent.
- Intentionally derailing issues, pull requests, or discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.
